## What is Guitar Rig?

**Native Instruments Guitar Rig** is a professional-grade virtual guitar effects processor and amp simulation software. It offers an extensive collection of amplifiers, cabinets, effects, and routing options in a modular, user-friendly interface. Whether you're recording in a studio, performing live, or practicing at home, Guitar Rig delivers authentic, high-quality guitar and bass tones with unparalleled flexibility.

With Guitar Rig, musicians can:
- Emulate classic and modern guitar amps and effects.
- Build custom signal chains with drag-and-drop modules.
- Use built-in metronome, tuner, and recorder for practice.
- Integrate seamlessly with DAWs via VST, AU, or AAX.

Its advanced sound engine and intuitive design make it a top choice for guitarists, producers, and sound engineers worldwide.
